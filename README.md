# MindCare - AI-Enhanced Student Counselling System

## Overview
MindCare is a **student counselling platform** that combines **AI-driven instant support** with **human counsellor guidance**. Students can share their problems and receive **immediate supportive messages from AI**, while counsellors provide personalized advice. All messages include **date and time stamps** for clarity and progress tracking.

---

## Features

### Student
- Login/Register securely
- Send messages about personal problems
- Receive **instant AI supportive responses** from a database of 500+ responses
- View **counsellor replies**
- **Message history** with **date and time** stamps

### Counsellor
- Login/Register securely
- View messages from students
- Respond to students with personalized guidance
- **Track student progress** through message history with timestamps

### AI Support
- Provides instant **emotional support** to students
- Matches student queries to pre-written responses
- Reduces waiting time for professional counselling

---

## How It Works
1. Student logs in and sends a message.
2. AI automatically responds with a supportive message, with **current date and time** recorded.
3. Counsellor reviews messages and sends personalized guidance, also timestamped.
4. Student sees both AI and counsellor responses with **time & date** in the chat history.

---

## Technologies Used
- Backend: Node.js 
- Database: MongoDB 
- AI: Pre-written responses (500+ messages) with keyword/sentiment matching
- Frontend: HTML/CSS/JS
